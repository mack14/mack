Welcome To Red2Roast

Red 2 Roast Limited was incorporated as a limited liability company in 2018. The company specializes in producing high quality roasted Arabica coffees sourced directly from a variety of farmers across the Rwenzori mountain ranges in western Uganda. From green bean to roasted coffee, our products and services cover the entire spectrum of the coffee value chain giving the customer an ideal opportunity to benefit from the journey made by the beans. Our goal is to be regarded as a great business partner and market leader in service and quality for specialty roasted coffees from Uganda and our objective is to build a sustainable coffee company using industry best practice that meets the daily needs of our consumers.

Products

The roasted coffees are carefully picked from specific single origins and processed to the highest quality. The coffee is then packed as either ground or whole beans in air tight zip locked coffee bags fitted with one-way de-gassing valves.
